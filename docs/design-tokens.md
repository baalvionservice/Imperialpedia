# Design Tokens

Design tokens are the visual atoms of our system. They are defined in `src/design-system/tokens/`.

## Colors
We use a sophisticated dark-themed palette by default.
- **Primary**: Distinguished Violet (`#8272F2`) - used for primary actions.
- **Secondary**: Sky Blue (`#69B9FF`) - used for accents and secondary highlights.
- **Neutral**: Purplish Grays - used for backgrounds, cards, and text.

## Spacing
Based on a 4px (0.25rem) grid.
- `xs`: 0.25rem (4px)
- `sm`: 0.5rem (8px)
- `md`: 1rem (16px)
- `lg`: 1.5rem (24px)
- `xl`: 2rem (32px)

## Radius
- `sm`: 0.125rem
- `md`: 0.375rem
- `lg`: 0.5rem (Standard for cards/buttons)
- `full`: 9999px

## Shadows
Used for elevation and depth.
- `sm`, `md`, `lg`, `xl`
